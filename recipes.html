Vegetable Roti Recipe

Desciption:

This is a healthy roti that is perfect for anytime, which is made with whole wheat flour and mix of vegetables. You can pair this delicious roti with plain curd.

Ingredients:

1 Cup atta; 1 cup mixed vegetables, boiled; 1 tsp roasted jeera powder; 1.5 tsp black pepper powder; 1 tsp lemon juice; 1 tbsp onion, chopped; 2 green chillies, finely chopped; 1 tsp coriander leaves; Black salt and salt, as per taste; 1/2 tsp oil

Method: 

                1.Take the boiled mixed vegetables in a bowl.
                2.Add all the ingredients (except atta and oil) and mash well.
                3.Add atta to it and knead with luke warm water.
                4.Cut small roundels and hand-press into rotis.
                5.Brush some oil and toast well on medium to low flame.
                6.Enjoy hot with dahi.

Atta Vegetable Khichdi

Description:

You must have tried many Khichdi recipes till now, but today we are going to share with you an interesting recipe of Atta Vegetable Khichdi. This is a nutritious khichdi recipe prepared with a mixture of wheat flour and vegetables.

Ingredients:

Cup whole wheat flour (atta), Assorted vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.), 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter), 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 1/2 tsp cumin powder, 1/2 tsp coriander powder, Salt to taste, Water

Method: 

                1.In a mixing bowl, add the whole wheat flour (atta). 
                2.Gradually add water while kneading the flour until you achieve a smooth and consistent paste. Set this aside. 
                3.Heat ghee in a deep, heavy-bottomed pan. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle. 
                4.Saute the vegetables until they turn slightly tender 
                5.Add the spices. Stir well to coat the vegetables with these flavourful spices. 
                6.Now, add the atta paste you prepared earlier to the saut√©ed vegetables. 
                7.Mix everything thoroughly to ensure the paste blends well with the vegetables and spices. 
                8.Pour enough water into the pan to cover the mixture. The water-to-atta ratio is usually 2:1. 
                9.Season with salt to taste. Allow the mixture to come to a gentle boil. 
                10.Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and let it simmer for about 15-20 minutes. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking. 
                11.Once your Atta Vegetable Khichdi is cooked to perfection, drizzle it with a little ghee for added flavour and richness. 
                12.Serve it hot.

Makhmali paneer tikka Recipe

Description:

Makhmali Paneer Tikka is a wonderful recipe. You can make this in two ways, if you want, you can stuffed a delicious filling between the paneer pieaces. In another method you can simply marinate paneer cubes and bake them in tandoor or oven. Serve with onions and green chutney.

Ingredients:

gms Paneer; 200 gram Hung Curd; 1 tbsp Fresh Cream; 2 tbsp Kaju Paste; 1 tsp Black Pepper Powder; A pinch of Haldi; 1 tsp Green Chilli Paste; to taste Salt; 2 tbsp Cheese, grated; 1 tbsp Roasted Besan; 1/4 cup Onion finely chopped; 1/4 cup Capsicum, finely chopped; 1/4 cup Red Bell Pepper, finely chopped; 2 tbsp Paneer, grated; 2 tbsp Coriander Leaves, finely chopped; A pinch of Cardamom Powder; 1 tsp Kasuri Methi Powder; 1/2 tsp Red Chilli Poweder; to taste Salt1 tbsp Cashew, finely chopped; 1 tsp Ghee; 1 tbsp Oil

Method: 

                1.First of all we have to prepare the marinade. Add hung curd, cashew paste, chili paste, roast gram flour, cream, cheese and other ingredients in a bowl and mix well and keep it aside. 
                2.Now, put a pan on the gas and heat it by adding oil, now fry finely chopped garlic, ginger and green chillies in it for a few seconds. After some time add onion, capsicum and yellow bell pepper and fry them. 
                3.When it is fried, take it out in a bowl and cool it. Now add grated paneer, cheese, kasoori methi and all other ingredients and mix well. Keep the stuffing aside. 
                4.Take paneer and cut it by making cuts from one side, so that it is attached to the side so that the stuffing does not come out. 
                5.Cut all the paneer pieces equally and now open the paneer piece from the middle and fill the stuffing. 
                6.Coat all these pieces well in the marinade. Now take a skewer, put capsicum and onion pieces in it, then Paneer. Place all the pieces in this order and bake in tandoor or oven for 10 to 15 minutes by applying a little ghee. 
                7.Makhmali Paneer Tikka is ready to serve. Pair with green chutney and onions rings.
